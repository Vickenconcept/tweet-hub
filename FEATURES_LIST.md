# XEngager  - Complete Feature List

## Overview
XEngager  is a comprehensive Twitter (X) content management and engagement platform that helps users create, schedule, and manage their Twitter presence efficiently. The app combines content creation tools, automation features, and analytics to streamline Twitter management.

---

## Core Features

### 1. **Chat / Compose**
**What it does:** The main content creation hub where users write and publish tweets.

**How it works:**
- Write single tweets or create multi-part threads
- Add images to posts
- Save drafts automatically as you type
- Post immediately or schedule for later
- View and manage drafts, scheduled posts, and sent posts in separate tabs
- Generate AI images directly within the composer
- Upload and reuse media from your asset library

**Key benefits:** One central place to create all your Twitter content with media support and scheduling options.

---

### 2. **Dashboard**
**What it does:** The main overview page that provides quick access to all features and shows account status.

**How it works:**
- Displays your connected Twitter account information
- Provides quick navigation cards to all major features
- Shows account connection status
- Central hub for accessing different sections of the app

**Key benefits:** Easy navigation and quick overview of your account status and available tools.

---

### 3. **Daily Ideas**
**What it does:** Automatically generates 12 ready-to-post tweets every day based on your topic and niche preferences.

**How it works:**
- Set your preferred topic (what you post about) and niche (your account type)
- Ideas are automatically generated daily and cached for the day
- Each idea is a complete, ready-to-post tweet (not just outlines)
- Ideas are tailored to your niche and include relevant hashtags
- Save favorite ideas for later use
- Edit ideas before posting or send them directly to the composer

**Key benefits:** Never run out of content ideas. Get fresh, relevant post suggestions every day without manual brainstorming.

---

### 4. **Generate Ideas**
**What it does:** On-demand content idea generator that creates tweet ideas based on your custom prompts.

**How it works:**
- Enter a specific prompt describing what kind of content you want
- Choose the type of ideas (general, tips, questions, etc.) and tone (professional, casual, etc.)
- Generate multiple ideas instantly
- Ideas are saved and can be accessed later
- Save favorites for future reference
- Edit ideas or send them directly to the composer

**Key benefits:** Create content ideas whenever you need them, tailored to specific topics or campaigns.

---

### 5. **Queued Posts**
**What it does:** Manage all your scheduled tweets in one place - view, edit, or delete posts before they go live.

**How it works:**
- View all scheduled posts in chronological order
- See when each post is scheduled to publish
- Edit post content or scheduled time
- Delete posts before they're published
- Posts are automatically published at their scheduled time

**Key benefits:** Full control over your scheduled content. Make last-minute changes or cancel posts before they go live.

---

### 6. **Mentions**
**What it does:** Monitor and respond to tweets that mention your Twitter account.

**How it works:**
- View all recent tweets that mention your account
- See who mentioned you and when
- Reply directly to mentions from within the app
- Like or retweet mentions with one click
- Auto-reply feature can automatically respond to mentions using AI (when enabled)
- Filter out duplicate mentions
- Pagination to browse through many mentions

**Key benefits:** Never miss a conversation. Engage with your audience quickly and efficiently, or let AI handle responses automatically.

---

### 7. **Keyword Monitor**
**What it does:** Search Twitter for specific keywords, hashtags, or phrases and monitor conversations about topics you care about.

**How it works:**
- Add keywords or hashtags to monitor (e.g., "your brand name", "#yourhashtag")
- View tweets containing your monitored keywords
- Reply, like, or retweet tweets directly from search results
- Use advanced search with filters (language, user, exclude retweets, etc.)
- Auto-reply feature can automatically respond to relevant tweets (when enabled)
- Search results are cached to reduce API usage

**Key benefits:** Track conversations about your brand, competitors, or industry topics. Engage with relevant discussions automatically.

---

### 8. **Tweet Analytics**
**What it does:** Analyze your tweets to see who liked them, who quoted them, and what replies they received.

**How it works:**
- Enter a tweet ID or select from your recent tweets
- View who liked your tweet
- See all quote tweets (when others quote your tweet)
- View all replies to your tweet
- Browse tweets you've liked
- Data is cached to reduce API calls

**Key benefits:** Understand your tweet performance and identify engaged followers. See who's interacting with your content.

---

### 9. **My Assets**
**What it does:** Central library for storing and managing images you use in tweets.

**How it works:**
- Upload images to your library
- Files are stored securely in the cloud
- Each asset gets a unique code for easy insertion into tweets
- Browse all your uploaded media
- Delete assets you no longer need
- Assets can be reused across multiple tweets

**Key benefits:** Build a reusable media library. No need to re-upload the same images repeatedly.

---

### 10. **Auto Daily Posts**
**What it does:** Automatically generates and posts content daily for business accounts based on configured profiles.

**How it works:**
- Create business profiles with specific topics, niches, and posting preferences
- Set whether to include AI-generated images with posts
- System automatically generates content daily for each active profile
- Posts are scheduled and published automatically
- View generated posts before they go live
- Regenerate posts if needed

**Key benefits:** Maintain consistent daily posting without manual work. Perfect for businesses that need regular content.

---

### 11. **Auto DMs**
**What it does:** Automatically send direct messages to selected Twitter users using customizable templates.

**How it works:**
- Create a campaign with a name and message template
- Search for Twitter users to message (by keyword, hashtag, or username)
- Select users from search results
- Set a daily limit for how many DMs to send
- Use AI to generate message templates based on your prompt
- Send test DMs to yourself before launching
- Messages are sent automatically in the background

**Key benefits:** Reach out to potential followers or customers automatically. Scale your outreach efforts efficiently.

---

### 12. **Twitter Settings**
**What it does:** Configure automatic engagement settings and manage your Twitter account connection.

**How it works:**
- Enable or disable automatic replies to mentions and keyword matches
- Set daily limits for automatic comments/replies
- View how many comments you've posted today
- See when your last automatic comment was sent
- Test your Twitter connection
- View your connected Twitter username

**Key benefits:** Control your automation settings. Prevent over-posting and manage your engagement limits.

---

## Additional Features

### **AI Image Generation**
**What it does:** Create custom images for your tweets using AI based on text descriptions.

**How it works:**
- Enter a description of the image you want
- AI generates an image matching your description
- Generated images are automatically saved to your asset library
- Use generated images immediately in your tweets

**Key benefits:** Create unique visuals for your content without design skills or stock photo subscriptions.

---

### **Thread Creation**
**What it does:** Create multi-part tweet threads (connected tweets) easily.

**How it works:**
- Start a thread by adding multiple messages
- Each message becomes a separate tweet that replies to the previous one
- Edit or remove individual messages before posting
- Post threads immediately or schedule them
- Threads maintain proper order when scheduled

**Key benefits:** Share longer thoughts or stories that exceed a single tweet's character limit.

---

### **Draft Management**
**What it does:** Automatically saves your work as you type, so you never lose content.

**How it works:**
- Content is saved automatically as you type
- Access all your drafts from the drafts tab
- Continue editing drafts anytime
- Delete drafts you no longer need

**Key benefits:** Never lose your work. Pick up where you left off on any tweet.

---

### **Timezone Support**
**What it does:** Schedule posts in your local timezone for easier planning.

**How it works:**
- Set your preferred timezone in settings
- All scheduled times display in your timezone
- Posts are automatically converted to the correct time for publishing

**Key benefits:** Schedule posts without timezone confusion. See times in a format that makes sense to you.

---

### **Auto-Reply Intelligence**
**What it does:** Automatically responds to mentions and keyword matches using AI-generated replies.

**How it works:**
- Enable auto-reply in Twitter Settings
- Set daily limits for automatic responses
- System generates contextually appropriate replies
- Only responds to tweets with questions (safety feature)
- Won't reply to your own tweets
- Won't reply twice to the same tweet
- Tracks which tweets have been auto-replied

**Key benefits:** Engage with your audience automatically while maintaining quality and avoiding spam.

---

### **Rate Limit Management**
**What it does:** Intelligently manages Twitter API usage to avoid hitting limits.

**How it works:**
- Caches data to reduce API calls
- Shows when rate limits are active
- Displays when rate limits reset
- Prevents unnecessary API calls when limits are reached
- Loads cached data when fresh data isn't available

**Key benefits:** Avoid service interruptions. The app works within Twitter's limits to ensure reliable operation.

---

## User Account Features

### **Twitter Account Connection**
**What it does:** Securely connect your Twitter account to enable all features.

**How it works:**
- Connect via official Twitter OAuth (secure login)
- Account information is synced automatically
- Profile picture and username displayed in sidebar
- Can disconnect and reconnect anytime
- Credentials are stored securely

**Key benefits:** Safe, official connection method. Your account stays secure while enabling powerful features.


## Summary

XEngager  provides a complete solution for Twitter management, combining:
- **Content Creation:** Write tweets, create threads, add media, generate ideas
- **Scheduling:** Plan and schedule posts in advance
- **Engagement:** Monitor mentions, track keywords, respond automatically
- **Analytics:** Understand your tweet performance
- **Automation:** Auto-post, auto-reply, auto-DM capabilities
- **Media Management:** Store and reuse images, videos, and GIFs
- **AI Assistance:** Generate content ideas and images using AI

All features work together to help users maintain an active, engaging Twitter presence with less manual effort.
